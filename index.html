Our Mission


We are Traible. Our goal is to help businesses save time by offering a software package that utilizes a smart database to capture the knowledge that experienced employees possess and make it available to all employees. We know that training new employees doesn’t always ensure that they are prepared for the variety of problems that they will face during the work day. An issue that would take an experienced employee very little time to solve, might take a newer employee much longer as they lack experience. Our program is a user-friendly database for storing and sharing all your important information. Traible will store: solutions, processes, formulas, important documents, and whatever you deem integral to your company. All of this then enables our AI to generate a solution based on the knowledge your experienced employees provide, and the document uploaded.
At Traible, our mission is to empower businesses to save time and enhance productivity by capturing the expertise of experienced employees and making it accessible to everyone. Through our intuitive software and AI-driven solutions, we ensure that critical knowledge—such as processes, solutions, and documents—is readily available, allowing teams to solve problems faster and work smarter. With Traible, we bridge the gap between new and seasoned employees, fostering a culture of shared knowledge and continuous improvement.

Function

The function of this invention is to close all gaps that are found in communication and/or training. Many companies are not able to provide suitable training due to information that is not shared but experienced. We want to take all the experiential knowledge and put it into software that makes it accessible to newer employees that are onboarding.


Operations

Traible is a software package that encompasses a user-friendly search engine (similar to how the Google search engine looks). An employee and/or individual would access the software that he downloaded to his computer and type in any issues that he may be experiencing. Once he is finished typing, (much like a search engine) the employee will hit enter and company-specific solution(s) will appear for them to use in resolving the issue they are experiencing.


Uses

Use: It can be used in all settings, including university settings. The goal is to erase the days of having tribal knowledge from tedious experiences to help with learning a certain system and instead make this genuine information more accessible to all individuals in their respective fields.
