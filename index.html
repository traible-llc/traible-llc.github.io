About Us

At Traible, we’re on a mission to revolutionize the way organizations capture, manage, and share internal knowledge.

Our platform is built to preserve the insights and problem-solving skills of experienced employees — ensuring that critical know-how never walks out the door.

Whether you’re onboarding new team members or optimizing workflows, Traible empowers your workforce by making expertise instantly searchable and accessible.

We believe in smarter work, fewer roadblocks, and faster resolutions — all backed by AI and powered by the people who know your company best.

Function

The function of Traible software is to close communication and training gaps. Many companies are not able to provide thorough training due to situational expeintial being required. We take all the experiential knowledge and put it into software that makes it accessible to new and seasoned employees.


Operations

Traible is a software package that encompasses a user-friendly search engine. Employees access the software and type in issues they may be experiencing. The awnsers and coaching will appear for them with company-specific solutions to use in resolving the issue.


Uses

Traible can be used in all knowledge transfer settings from the junior mechanics troubleshooting a machine to a consultant investigating historic work. Whether you're looking to streamline onboarding, provide instant troubleshooting, or preserve experiential knowledge, Traible adapts to your organization's unique needs, helping you save time, boost productivity, and maintain a competitive edge in your industry.
