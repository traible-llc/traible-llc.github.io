Changing How Businesses Transfer Knowledge


When systems go down, there’s no time to waste. Traible offers a powerful solution to make critical expertise accessible across your organization, ensuring that knowledge is just a search away. With its user-friendly, AI-driven platform, Traible captures the insights and skills of experienced employees, transforming them into searchable, actionable solutions for everyone.

Capturing and Democratizing Expertise


Traible allows businesses to safeguard essential knowledge, even when senior staff move on. By translating experience-based know-how into a structured format, Traible bridges gaps in communication and training, turning valuable, often implicit knowledge into a long-term asset. This approach makes expertise available when it’s needed most, reducing dependency on specific individuals and empowering teams with immediate access to solutions.

Benefits Beyond Onboarding


  • • Knowledge Retention: As seasoned employees retire or transition, their expertise stays within the organization, accessible to everyone.

  • • Enhanced Productivity: Close skill gaps and speed up problem resolution with instant access to specialized knowledge.

  • • Accelerated Onboarding: Blend digital training with real-world scenarios, cutting down on onboarding time and boosting confidence for new hires.

Function

The function of this invention is to close all gaps that are found in communication and/or training. Many companies are not able to provide suitable training due to information that is not shared but experienced. We want to take all the experiential knowledge and put it into software that makes it accessible to newer employees that are onboarding.


Operations

Traible is a software package that encompasses a user-friendly search engine (similar to how the typical search engine looks). An employee and/or individual would access the software that he downloaded to his computer and type in any issues that he may be experiencing. Once he is finished typing, (much like a search engine) the employee will hit enter and company-specific solution(s) will appear for them to use in resolving the issue they are experiencing.


Uses

Traible can be used in all knowledge transfer settings from the junior manufacturing mechanice troubleshooting a machine to a consultant investigating work done for a client years ago. Whether you're looking to streamline onboarding, provide instant troubleshooting, or preseve experiential knowledge, Traible adapts to your organization's unique needs, helping you save time, boost productivity, and maintain a competitive edge in your industry.
