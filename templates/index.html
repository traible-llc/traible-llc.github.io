Frequently Asked Questions

What is Traible and how does it work?

▼

Traible is an AI-powered platform that captures and organizes internal organizational knowledge, making it easily searchable and accessible. It ensures that critical expertise doesn't leave when experienced employees do.

Who can benefit from using Traible?

▼

Any organization looking to retain knowledge, improve onboarding, and reduce downtime. Whether you're in tech, manufacturing, healthcare, or education — Traible helps preserve expert insights.

Is my company's data secure on Traible?

▼

Yes. Security is our top priority. Traible uses industry-standard encryption and best practices to keep your data safe, private, and accessible only to your team.

How do I get started with Traible?

▼

You can request a demo or sign up for an onboarding session. We’ll guide you through setting up your knowledge base and training your team to get the most out of Traible.

Can Traible integrate with our existing tools?

▼

Absolutely. We offer integrations with popular collaboration platforms, databases, and file storage services. Custom integrations can also be developed based on your needs.
